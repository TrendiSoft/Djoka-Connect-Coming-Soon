ABOUT US

By setting up this international podium, Djoka Connect is pursuing the following missions:
- to allow a reliable contact and exchange with artists such as musicians, animators and dancers for different events
- allow even less well-known artists to make themselves more visible
- make it easier to find events such as concerts, dances, nightclubs, music festivals around us
- bring together people who want to live their passion "have fun together".

Djoka Connect is committed to doing everything in its power to protect its users' data and to respect ethics and professional conduct.
